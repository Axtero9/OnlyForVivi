# 🌿 Vivitoli's PCB Career Guide

A personalised, mobile-first career guide built for **Vivitoli Chishi** — a Class 11 PCB student who wants to earn in lakhs, have a life, and not spend 15 years studying.

---

## 📱 About

This is a single-file HTML website that maps out the best (and worst) career options after choosing PCB in Class 11. Careers are ranked from least to most suitable based on work-life balance, time to start earning, and salary potential.

Each career card includes:
- **What they do** — actual day-to-day work explained simply
- **Salary** — realistic monthly/annual numbers
- **Roadmap** — step-by-step path from Class 12 to earning

---

## 🗂️ Career Tiers

### 🟫 Least Suitable
| Career | Why it's tough |
|---|---|
| MBBS Doctor | 10–14 years of study, starts earning at 27 |
| BAMS Ayurveda | Low salaries unless you go luxury/private |

### 🟢 Mid Suitable
| Career | Highlight |
|---|---|
| Physiotherapy (BPT) | Sports rehab, own clinic, no NEET needed |
| Clinical Research (CRA) | Office job, pharma industry, ₹6–20 LPA |
| Nutritionist / Dietitian | Build online brand, consult from anywhere |

### ✦ Most Suitable
| Career | Why it's a W |
|---|---|
| Dentistry (BDS) | Own clinic, set your hours, ₹1–5L/mo |
| Healthcare Management | BSc + MBA = corporate healthcare, ₹15–40 LPA |
| Optometry | Most underrated pick — clinic + optical shop = ₹80k–2.5L/mo |
